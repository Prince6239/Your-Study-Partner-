🔬 SCIENCE STREAM



  1. 🛰 Physics

    2. 🔹 Important Chapters:

    • Electrostatics & Current Electricity

    • Magnetic Effects & EMI

    • Optics (Ray & Wave)

    • Modern Physics (Photoelectric Effect, Atoms, Nuclei)

    ✅ Tips:

    • Focus on derivations and conceptual clarity.

    • Practice numericals daily.

    • Prepare formula sheet and revise daily.

  2. 🧪 Chemistry

    3. 🔹 Important Chapters:

    • Electrochemistry, Surface Chemistry

    • d- and f-block elements

    • Organic Chemistry (Aldehydes, Ketones, Haloalkanes)

    • Biomolecules & Polymers (short and scoring)

    ✅ Tips:

    • Learn name reactions and mechanisms in Organic.

    • Practice balancing and numerical problems.

    • Use flowcharts for reactions and classification.

  3. 🧬 Biology

    4. 🔹 Important Chapters:

    • Human Reproduction & Reproductive Health

    • Genetics & Evolution

    • Biotechnology

    • Ecology (very scoring)

    ✅ Tips:

    • Revise diagrams and labelings.

    • Make short notes of key terms & cycles.

    • Solve sample questions after each topic.

  4. 📐 Mathematics

    5. 🔹 Important Chapters:

    • Relations & Functions, Inverse Trigonometry

    • Matrices & Determinants

    • Continuity, Differentiability, Integration

    • Probability, Linear Programming

    ✅ Tips:

    • Practice all types of questions from NCERT.

    • Write step-by-step solutions for theory-based questions.

    • Maintain a formula notebook.

  5. 💻 Computer Science / IP

    6. 🔹 Important Chapters:

    • Python Programming

    • MySQL Queries

    • File Handling, DBMS

    ✅ Tips:

    • Practice coding by writing and solving small problems.

    • Memorize syntax and logic.

    • Understand database concepts and practice SQL queries.
