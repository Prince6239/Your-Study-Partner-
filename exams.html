🎓 Exams After 12th

→ After Class 12, students appear for different competitive exams based on their stream (Science, Commerce, Arts) to get admission into colleges or professional courses.

🧪 Science – Non-Medical (PCM)

  • 📚 Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics

  • 🎓 Courses: Engineering, Architecture, Defense, Aviation, B.Sc.

  • 🔹 Top Exams:

    1. JEE Main & Advanced – For B.Tech/B.E. (IITs, NITs, IIITs)

    2. NDA (National Defence Academy) – For Indian Army, Navy, Air Force

    3. BITSAT – For BITS Pilani and other campuses

    4. VITEEE / SRMJEEE / COMEDK – Private university entrance tests

    5. NATA – For Architecture (B.Arch.)

    6. CUET (UG) – For admission to B.Sc., BCA, etc. in Central Universities

🧬 Science – Medical (PCB)

  • 📚 Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology

  • 🎓 Courses: MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, Nursing, B.Sc. (Biology)

  • 🔹 Top Exams:

    1. NEET UG – For MBBS, BDS, BAMS, B.Sc. Nursing

    2. AIIMS Nursing – B.Sc. (Hons) Nursing at AIIMS

    3. CUET (UG) – For biology-related courses in central universities

    4. ICAR AIEEA – For agricultural sciences

    5. CPNET / UPUMS – For paramedical and nursing programs

📊 Commerce

  • 📚 Subjects: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Maths

  • 🎓 Courses: CA, CS, BBA, B.Com, Management, Law

  • 🔹 Top Exams:

    1. CA Foundation – For Chartered Accountant

    2. CSEET – For Company Secretary

    3. CUET (UG) – For B.Com, BBA, B.A. Economics in central universities

    4. IPMAT – For 5-year Integrated MBA at IIM Indore, Rohtak

    5. DU JAT (now merged in CUET) – For Delhi University BMS/BBA

    6. SET – Symbiosis Entrance Test for BBA and Law

🎨 Arts / Humanities

  • 📚 Subjects: History, Political Science, Geography, Psychology, etc.

  • 🎓 Courses: Law, Design, Social Work, B.A., Journalism, Civil Services

  • 🔹 Top Exams:

    1. CUET (UG) – For B.A., Journalism, Sociology, etc.

    2. CLAT – Common Law Admission Test (5-year BA LLB)

    3. NID / NIFT – Design & Fashion Institutes

    4. TISS-BAT – B.A. in Social Sciences (Tata Institute of Social Sciences)

    5. AILET – National Law University Delhi

    6. IHM NCHMCT JEE – Hotel Management (open to all streams)

🌐 Open to All Streams

  • 🔸 These exams can be given by students of any stream:

    1. CUET (UG) – For all central university UG courses.

    2. SAT – For studying abroad

    3. IELTS / TOEFL – English language tests for international admissions

    4. TNDA – For defense services (PCM required for Air Force/ Navy)
